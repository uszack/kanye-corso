About Kanye Corso

I am a balanced dog trainer, with a background and understanding of behavioral analysis and predictive analysis. As a subject matter expert for a government agency, a police officer, a detective, and now Canine Behaviorist I have mastered a unique technique that enables me to restructure your dog's mind and focus it on you for guidance and direction in all settings!

If you dream of taking your dog to the park, the beach to BBQ with friends, or allowing friends and family to bring a dog over during the holidays, I am your solution.

  • I want to keep dogs out of shelters and away from being put to sleep.
  • I specialize in Large POWERFUL breed dogs like the Cane Corso, but I train ALL dogs, and I assist all people from all walks of life.

Why have a dog that is out of control, pulls on the leash, and doesn't stop barking when people come to the door? Have the dream dog you have always wanted, an Elite K9 Companion.
